Common checks

  • Health access can be managed in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Graft.
  • Screen Time and shield permissions can be managed in iOS Settings → Screen Time.
  • Subscriptions can be managed in iOS Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.
  • If a trusted workout has not appeared yet, open the recording app, let it sync to Apple Health, then return to Graft and use Check now.

What Graft can and cannot do

Graft is strong friction, not a tamper-proof lock against the device owner. It uses Apple Health, Screen Time, and StoreKit on the device; it does not run a developer-operated backend or require a Graft account.
